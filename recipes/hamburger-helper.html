Hamburger Helper Recipe

Description:

In the following sections we will be making a delicacy.

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger helper
  • Button mushrooms
  • Broccoli
  • Scorpion pepper hot sauce

Steps:

  1. Cut mushrooms to preference
  2. Sauté in a pan with butter
  3. Prep broccoli
  4. Cook hamburger helper to recipe, adding broccoli while simmering
  5. add hot sauce to preference
